Restoration and Dignity

The Donna Jean Legacy: Our Story

For over 30 years, my beautiful Mommy, Donna Jean Coleman-Scotti viewed her salon visits as a ritual of strength—a non-negotiable time for dignity and self-care. When an eight-year battle with multiple myeloma (a rare blood cancer) stripped that ritual away, the seed for this studio was planted.

Founded by her son, Victor Scotti, Jr., our clinic is a love letter to Donna's strength. We provide a sanctuary for those transitioning from "salon client" to "patient," ensuring your image remains a vital component of your healing.
